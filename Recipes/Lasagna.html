Lasagna Recipe


I'm a hater of 'saucy' foods but my housemates love it.

Recipe Source

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheeses of Choice

Recipe

  1. Make the Sauce

    Make the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.

  2. Simmer

    Put the sauce on some heat and leave it on a simmer.

  3. Boil the noodlesIn a separate pot, boil the noodles while watching the sauce simmer.
  4. ASSEMBLE!

    Start assembling the structure. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until out of ingredients!

  5. EAT
